Join us at any stage, The Black Female Founders Hub offers a
suite of programs designed to empower Black Female
Entrepreneurs at every stage of their journey.

Whether you are brimming with a brilliant business idea, actively
building your venture, or seeking to scale an existing enterprise,
we have the resources and support you need.

Advanced Founders
Program

This program is designed to propel organisations founded by Black women to new heights. It equips established entrepreneurs with the tools and resources they need to scale their businesses and empowers them to mentor the next generation of founders. Through the program, founders gain access to valuable connections, industry insights, and opportunities that generate lasting impact beyond the program’s duration

  • Coaching and mentorship opportunities.
  • Advanced Workshops
  • Networking with Industry Experts
  • Alumni Memberships

Incubate State
Program

This program provides mentorship, networking opportunities, funding, resources, and education to help early stage founders grow their businesses.
It is also designed to address gaps in areas of business skills and structures for early stage Founders’ growth and scalability.

  • Consultation with business experts on product-market fit, branding and marketing, revenue modelling & funding, intellectual property, and more
  • Access to a network of prospective partners and co-founders.
  • World-class opportunity to be mentored by advanced Founders.
  • Alumni Memberships
  • Progressing to an Accelerator Scheme

Studpreneur Program

This is a unique program designed for aspiring young entrepreneurs aged 16+. We take students and fresh graduates from where they are to where they want to be.We believe that you have a seed of greatness, and we nurture it for you to stand out in your industry, academics, and the emerging market.

  • World-class training from industry experts.
  • A resource centre that provides access to tools, research materials, legal and financial advice.
  • Provision of a community that supports, ranging from academic to professional, to stand out in the industry.
  • Opportunities for internships with industry experts
